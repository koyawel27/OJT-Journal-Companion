      Persistent storage

      Checking persistent-storage status...

      Persistent storage may reduce the chance of automatic browser eviction, but it does not replace JSON backups. Data can still be lost when browser data is cleared, the browser profile is removed, the device fails, or you switch devices.

      Recovery guidance

      • Your journal is stored locally in this browser profile. Clearing browser or site data, browser or device maintenance, or device failure may remove it.
      • Private or incognito browsing may use temporary storage that is removed when the private session ends; the app does not try to detect private browsing.
      • Switching browsers, browser profiles, or devices does not transfer local data. Cloud sync does not exist.
      • JSON is the app's portable recovery backup. Store regular backups outside the browser or device when practical.
      • DOCX exports are editable journal documents for review, sharing, or printing. They cannot restore app data.
      • Persistent storage may reduce browser eviction risk, but it cannot prevent all data loss and does not replace JSON backups.
      Danger zone

      Reset App Data

      Your journal lives only in this browser. Reset permanently removes all local OJT data — profile, weeks, daily logs, tasks, photos, and settings. Export a backup first if you may need it later.
